    • Texas
      • Lufkin Lodge #669 is collecting donation items for the Texas Masonic Retirement Center. They will be collecting clothing, personal items, movies, and medical equipment. They are looking for personal care items like toothpaste, toothbrushes, shampoo, body wash, shaving items, and beauty products. Additionally, they are looking for puzzles, playing cards, and games - especially things like large-print crossword puzzle books. Books, music, and movies are appreciated as well. Please make sure that clothing and shoes are new or gently-used. If you've got wheelchairs, walkers, IV stands, boots, etc., that you don't need anymore, please consider donating those, too.
      • April 29th - Lyons Jr. Lodge is doing a custom car and bike show in Austin at 10 am. It's at the George Washington Carver Museum and Cultural Center in Austin. Entry is $25, registration is at 10 am, and judging begins at high twelve. There is are door prizes and a free t-shirt with entry. Call 512-845-5549 for more info.
      • April 29th - Huffman Masonic Lodge #1428 is having a clayshoot at the Clay Mound Sporting Center in Liberty, Texas. Registration is at 8 am, start is at 9, all cards must be in at 3 pm for the 4 pm awards. Lunch is included, and there will be a silent auction. 100 clays are $90/shooter and mulligans are $5 a pop for a max of four mulligans. Teal of Terror is $10/attempt. The highest overall award is a custom belt buckle, and there are varying awards for different age groups.
      • May 6th - Chalk Mountain Lodge #894 is holding a fish fry fundraiser from 4 PM to 7 PM. Tickets are ten dollars for adults and $5 for children 10 years or under. All proceeds are going to the Chalk Mountain Lodge Charitable Works Fund. If you want to join them, the event is happening at 244 South Columbia in Stephenville.
