  • Events
    • State
      • April 8th - Longview Lodge #404 in Longview is hosting a combination blood drive/fish fry. The blood drive will be from 10 am to 2 pm, and the fish fry will be from 11 am to 2 pm. The blood drive is for the Gift of Life Program and the Fish Fry is for the Longiew Masonic Library & Museum.
    • Tarrant County / Nearby
      • April 8th - Lufkin Lodge #669 is putting on a scholarship golf tournament. The tournament is taking places at Neches Pines -Diboli, registration is at 8 AM, and start is at 9 AM. Cost for a four-person team is $300, and cost for a corporate team is $500. Multiple contests are going to be held and there are several levels of sponsonship available. For more details, please check out the Lufkin Lodge #669 AF&AM facebook page.
      • April 8th - Webb Lodge (#1454) is putting on an outdoor Master Mason's degree.
        • Western attire is encouraged and bringing your own chair and your own apron is required.
        • The Hella Shrine Knights of Mecca Degree Team is conferring.
        • The location is 32900 US 281 in Lipan, TX (zipcode 76462), which is 10.25 miles south of I-20, on the East side of US 281.
        • In the case of bad weather, the degree will be relocated to Harmony Lodge #592 in Morgan Mill
        • Lunch is at 11:30 AM and the degree itself is at 1 PM. Suggested donation for lunch is $15 - help a brother out!
        • Webb Lodge is in need of a multi-passenger UTV, so please, if you have one that you can loan to them and you're within your cabletow's length, please reach out to them.
        • RSVP to Brother Senior Warden Mark Myers at webblodge1454@gmail.com or 817-253-0964
      • April 9th - Hella Shrine in Garland is holding a crawfish boil fundraiser. The event is from noon to five and the $20 tickets must be reserved in advance. You can BYOB in addition to the beer and soda that is already at the event. To reserve tickets, call John Krodel at 214-762-6077 or the Hella Shrine Temple at 972-240-6521. Hella Shrine is located at 2121 Rowlett Road in Garland, Texas.
      • April 13th - Handley Lodge (#1140) has their stated meetings on the second Thursday of every month at 7:30 PM. If they still have the travelling gavel and the Mega Gavel by then, bring seven or more brothers to their meeting, and claim it for your lodge! Remember to email the 64th District with an update as to who now has the Travelling Gavel.
    • Temple - 1100 Henderson Street in Fort Worth
      • Tuesday, April 11th - Texas Council is putting on the Royal Master and Select Master Degrees. Please come to that, too! As with all of the Texas Chapter and Council events, this is in the Third Floor Red Room.
    • As always, Fort Worth #148 meets on the Second Monday of each month, starting dinner at 6PM for a 7PM stated meeting at 1100 Henderson Street, Fort Worth, Texas
