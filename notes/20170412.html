      • Greensboro, North Carolina - a bunch of go-karts were stolen from Oasis Shrine in Greensboro on either the fifth or sixth of April. The thieves took an entire trailer full of go-karts. The trailer is white with big lettering on the side that says "GREENSBORO NC", "NASCARTS", AND "OASIS SHRINERS." If you're in Guilford County, and you see that trailer, please contact the Sheriff's office.
      • Beverley Hills, California - Brother Don Rickles has laid down his working tools. He passed away from kidney failure on April 6th at the age of 90. He is best known for his stand-up comedy and he was raised to the sublime degreee of a master mason on June 6th, 1953, by Service City Geba Lodge #1009 in Astoria, New York.
      • Louisville, Kentucky - hundreds of Masons gathered together on April 8th to form the largest human square and compasses. This occured during the 14th annual Great Day of Service when KY masons do service work for the local masonic retirement homes.
    • Abroad
      • UGLE - three brothers from the UGLE were interviewed on BBC Breakfast this morning! They were featured on the documentary "Inside the Freemasons." The first episode airs on Monday of next week on Sky 1 HD. Hopefully it's good!
