      • April 29th - Lyons Jr. Lodge is doing a custom car and bike show in Austin at 10 am. It's at the George Washington Carver Museum and Cultural Center in Austin. Entry is $25, registration is at 10 am, and judging begins at high twelve. There is are door prizes and a free t-shirt with entry. Call 512-845-5549 for more info.
    • Tarrant County / Nearby
      • April 13th - Burleson Lodge #649 is going to put on the first of two Master's degrees, with the second occuring on April 20th. Meal is at 6 PM, degree beginning at 7 PM. If you can mae it to one or both, they would love that. For more info, and to let their Secretary, Brother Rodney Beckman, know you're coming, please give them a call at 817-648-1373.
      • April 13th - Handley Lodge (#1140) has their stated meetings on the second Thursday of every month at 7:30 PM. If they still have the travelling gavel and the Mega Gavel by then, bring seven or more brothers to their meeting, and claim it for your lodge! Remember to email the 64th District with an update as to who now has the Travelling Gavel.
      • May 20th - this is a little in advance, but I wanted to toss this one out there since tickets are going pretty quick. The Oklahoma Indian Degree team is coming to Garland, Texas! The MWSA of Masonic District #9 and East Fork Lodge #650 are hosting a Master Mason's Degree conferred by the Oklahoma Indian Degree team.
        • Again, the date is Saturday, May 20th, at 1 pm following the 11:30 am lunch.
        • The location is Black Horse Barn at Hella Shrine, 2121 Rowlett Road in Garland, Texas.
        • Tickets are $25, which includes lunch.
        • Seats are limited to 500
        • EventBrite.com E V E N T B R I T E .COM
        • www.eastforklodge.org/facebook
        • Open to master masons only (of course)

    • Temple - 1100 Henderson Street in Fort Worth
      • Saturday, April 15th - Worth Commandery is putting on the Orders. It's gonna be an all-day affair. The degrees start at 8 am after breakfast and should theoretically conclude at around 4. I'll be one of the candidates. Please come if you are a Sir Knight!
      • Monday, April 17th - Fort Worth 148 is putting on a Master Mason's degree at 6! Please come join us.
      • Monday, April 24th - Brother Roberto Sanchez is coming to the temple at 6 pm to speak on "The True Masonic Experience." Brother Sanchez is a Masonic speaker and author who has lectured all over the US, Canada, Latin America, and Europe. This event is open to all regular masons, including Entered Apprentices and Fellowcrafts - please join us!
      • Monday, May 1st - Fort Worth 148 is putting on another Master Mason's degree at 6! Please come join us.
